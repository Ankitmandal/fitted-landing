Install Fitted

Takes about a minute. No coding required.

Download fitted-v0.1.0.zip
1
Unzip the file you just downloaded. You'll get a folder called fitted-v0.1.0.
2
Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions
3
Turn on Developer mode — it's a toggle in the top-right corner. Completely safe, just lets you load your own extensions.
4
Click Load unpacked and select the fitted-v0.1.0 folder you unzipped.
5
Sign in with Google, upload your resume (.docx), and start browsing jobs. Fitted will appear automatically on LinkedIn, Naukri, Indeed, and more.
