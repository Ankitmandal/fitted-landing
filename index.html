How it works

Three steps. One browser tab.

Upload once, browse jobs, get instant analysis. No copy-pasting into ChatGPT, no prompt engineering.

1
Upload your resume
Drop your DOCX, PDF, or TXT file — or paste text directly. Parsed locally in your browser, never leaves your device.
2
Browse any job listing
LinkedIn, Indeed, Naukri, Glassdoor, or any career page. Fitted auto-detects the job description and analyzes it against your resume, requirement by requirement.
3
Accept or reject each change
Review per-bullet rewrites with full reasoning. Accept what works, reject what doesn't. Export as DOCX, PDF, or plain text in your chosen template.
Features

Built for people who are tired of the guesswork.

Every feature exists because generic resume advice doesn't work. The JD tells you exactly what to say — Fitted reads it so you don't have to.

Requirement-by-requirement analysis
Every JD requirement mapped against your resume. Green = covered. Amber = your experience applies but the framing is wrong. Red = genuine gap.
Per-bullet refactoring with reasoning
Each bullet shows why it was changed and what it now signals. Reframed, refined, compressed, or added — with the original always visible side-by-side.
Multi-format input
Upload DOCX (recommended), PDF, or TXT. Or paste your resume text directly. All parsed into the same structured format locally.
