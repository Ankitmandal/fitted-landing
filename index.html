Fitted

You're probably qualified for more jobs than you think.

Most resumes get rejected not because you lack the skills — but because the way you describe them doesn't match how the job description asks for them.

Fitted is a Chrome extension that reads the JD, compares it against your resume line by line, and shows you exactly where you stand. When there's a gap, it's usually not missing experience — it's missing framing.

I built this because I got tired of the guesswork.
