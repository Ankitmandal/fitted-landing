How it works
Three steps. Zero setup.
Upload once, then browse jobs as you normally would. Fitted does the rest.
Step 1

Upload your resume once

Upload your DOCX resume in the extension settings. It's parsed locally and stored in your browser.

Step 2

Browse job listings as usual

Visit any job page on LinkedIn, Indeed, Naukri, or Glassdoor. Fitted auto-detects the job description.

Step 3

See your match + get a tailored resume

Instant grade, requirement-by-requirement analysis, and AI-refactored bullet points you accept or reject.
