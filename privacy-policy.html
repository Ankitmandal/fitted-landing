Fitted — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

What we collect

  • Email address — provided via Google sign-in, used for account identification.
  • Usage statistics — number of detections and refactorings performed, stored in Firebase Firestore.
  • Account plan — your current plan tier (e.g., "free"), stored in Firebase Firestore.

What we process

When you analyze a job listing, the job description text and your CV text are sent to our server for processing. This data is processed in real time and immediately discarded after the response is returned. We do not store CV content, job description content, or refactoring results on our servers.

Third-party services

  • Groq — LLM provider used to analyze job descriptions and generate refactoring suggestions. Job description and CV text are sent to Groq's API for processing.
  • Firebase Authentication — Google sign-in for account management.
  • Firebase Firestore — stores your email, usage counts, and account plan.

What we store

  • Your email address
  • Usage counts (detections per day, refactorings per month)
  • Account plan information
  • Analysis metadata (role title, company, match grade — no CV or JD content)

What we do NOT store

  • Your CV content
  • Job description text
  • Refactoring results or suggestions

Data retention

Account data is retained as long as your account exists. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.
